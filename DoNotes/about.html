About DoNotes

DoNotes is your all-in-one productivity tool that seamlessly blends note-taking and to-do lists in a single, easy-to-use platform.

🎯 Our Mission

To help you stay focused, organized, and productive — whether you're managing daily tasks, planning projects, or capturing big ideas.

🚀 Key Features

  • 📝 Simple and powerful note editor
  • ✅ Smart to-do list with reminders
  • 🔄 Sync across devices
  • 🎨 Clean, distraction-free interface

👨‍💻 Built For

Students, professionals, creatives — anyone who needs a better way to think, plan, and get things done.
