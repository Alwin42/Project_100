Payment Issues & Failed Transactions

My payment was declined - what should I do?
  • Check if your card has enough money or isn't expired
  • Make sure you entered the correct card details (number, CVV, expiry date)
  • Contact your bank - they might have blocked the transaction for security
  • Try a different payment method (different card, UPI, net banking)

Payment deducted but order not confirmed

  • Don't worry! Your money is safe and will be returned within 3-5 business days
  • Submit a ticket with your transaction ID for faster resolution
  • Check your email for any confirmation messages
